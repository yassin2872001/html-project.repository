Some of the most common forms of mobile computing devices are as given below: Portable computers, compact, lightweight units including a full character set keyboard and primarily intended as hosts forsoftware that may be parameterized, such as laptops/desktops, smartphones/tablets, etc.

    * Smart cards that can run multiple applications but are typically used for payment, travel and secure area access.

   * Mobile phones, telephony devices which can call from a distance through cellular networking technology.

   * Wearable computers, mostly limited to functional keys and primarily intended as incorporation of software agents, such as bracelets, key-less implants, etc..
